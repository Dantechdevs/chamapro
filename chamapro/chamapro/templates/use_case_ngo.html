The Accountability Challenge

NGOs and community groups are trusted with other people's money. Donors, members, and beneficiaries all expect transparency — but most groups are still managing funds in cash, notebooks, or basic spreadsheets.

When records are informal, trust breaks down. Disputes arise, audits fail, and donors lose confidence. ChamaPro gives your group the professional financial infrastructure it deserves.

No audit trail for donor accountability

Features Built for Community Accountability

Simple tools that make your group trustworthy and professional.

Full Transaction Audit Trail

Every contribution, disbursement, and loan is recorded with the date, amount, member, and the staff member who entered it. Nothing can be quietly changed.

Donor-Ready Reports

Generate professional PDF and Excel financial summaries in one click. Share with donors, auditors, or your board — no formatting required.

Role-Based Access

Programme officers, finance staff, and volunteers each get the right level of access. No accidental or unauthorised changes.

M-Pesa Collection

Collect member contributions via M-Pesa STK Push. Payments are automatically recorded — no cash to count, no receipts to chase.

Multiple Groups

Run multiple beneficiary groups or projects under one organisation account. Each group has its own records, members, and reports.

Welfare & Emergency Loans

Track emergency disbursements and welfare loans with repayment schedules. Every shilling disbursed is accounted for.
