The Challenge Banks Face

Financial institutions serving Chama clients face a recurring problem — their clients are managing hundreds of thousands of shillings in spreadsheets, notebooks, and WhatsApp groups. This creates risk for the bank and frustration for the client.

When Chama records are informal, loan applications are slower, audits are harder, and compliance reporting becomes a manual headache for your relationship managers.

Manual record-keeping creates audit gaps
Relationship managers spend hours reconciling records
Loan eligibility is hard to verify without digital records
No visibility into group financial health

How ChamaPro Solves It

ChamaPro gives your institution a white-label digital platform that your Chama clients use to manage their own records — while giving your team the oversight and reporting tools you need.

Full digital audit trail for every transaction
Relationship managers get group financial dashboards
Loan history and contribution records for credit scoring
Compliance-ready PDF and Excel exports
API integration with your core banking system
