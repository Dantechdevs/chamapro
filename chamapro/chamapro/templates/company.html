About ChamaPro

We're on a mission to bring every Chama in Africa into the digital age — one group at a time.

Our Mission

To empower every informal investment group in Africa with simple, affordable digital tools — so they can focus on growing wealth, not managing paperwork.

Our Vision

A future where every Chama — from a 5-member village group to a 500-member Sacco — has access to the same quality of financial management tools as the world's largest institutions.

Our Story

ChamaPro was born out of a frustration we all recognised — watching Chama treasurers spend hours every month with exercise books, calculators, and WhatsApp screenshots trying to track who paid and who didn't.

Our founders, all members of Chamas themselves, set out to build the tool they wished they had — something simple enough for a first-time smartphone user, powerful enough for a Sacco managing hundreds of members.

What started as a side project in Nairobi in 2020 has grown into a platform trusted by thousands of groups across all 47 counties of Kenya — with expansion into Uganda and Tanzania underway.
