Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

How do I add members to my Chama?
Go to your Chama's Members page and click "Invite Member". Enter the email address of the person you want to add. They must already have a ChamaPro account. Once added, they will appear in your member list immediately.
How does M-Pesa payment work?
When you initiate a payment, an STK Push notification is sent to your phone. Enter your M-Pesa PIN to confirm. The payment is automatically recorded in your Chama's contribution history once confirmed by Safaricom.
Can I export my Chama's financial records?
Yes. From the Reports section, you can export your financial data as PDF, Excel (.xlsx), or CSV. Exports include contributions, loans, member summaries, and financial overviews.
What happens if I forget my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your registered email address. You will receive a password reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it in your inbox.
Can I have multiple Chamas on one account?
Yes. A single ChamaPro account can belong to multiple Chamas. You can switch between them from your dashboard. Each Chama has its own contributions, loans, and member records.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel your subscription from the Chama Settings page under the Billing section. Your account will remain active until the end of the current billing period. No refunds are issued for unused time.
Is my financial data secure?
Yes. ChamaPro uses industry-standard encryption for all data. We never share your financial data with third parties. Backups are performed regularly to prevent data loss.
How do I delete my account?
To request account deletion, email us at info@chamapro.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request". Include your registered email address. We will process your request within 7 business days and send a confirmation.
