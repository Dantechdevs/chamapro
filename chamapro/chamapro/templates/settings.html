Role Definitions

Four built-in roles — each with different levels of access.

Admin
  • Full access to all features
  • Manage members & roles
  • Approve loans & withdrawals
  • Edit chama settings
  • Delete / archive chama
Treasurer
  • Record contributions
  • Approve / reject loans
  • Manage fines
  • View financial reports
  • Cannot edit settings
Secretary
  • Invite & remove members
  • Send group messages
  • Record meeting minutes
  • View all reports
  • Cannot manage finances
Member
  • View own contributions
  • Request loans
  • View group balance
  • Receive messages
  • No management access
