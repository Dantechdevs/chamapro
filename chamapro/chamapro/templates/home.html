About ChamaPro

Smart Contributions Management for Your Chama

ChamaPro helps investment groups and Chamas manage contributions, loans, and group records with ease. Our system ensures transparency, accountability, and convenience for all members.

ChamaPro was created to empower Chamas and investment groups by providing a modern, digital way to manage contributions without stress. We believe in accountability, financial growth, and easy collaboration among members.

Our mission is to simplify group savings and contributions through technology. We aim to ensure transparency, trust, and efficiency in every Chama across Kenya.

We envision a future where Chamas operate like professional financial institutions using affordable, accessible tools — making every contribution count towards financial freedom.
