About Chamapro

Smart Contributions Management for Your Chama

Chamapro helps investment groups and Chamas manage contributions, loans, and group records with ease. Our system ensures transparency, accountability, and convenience for all members. Whether you are saving, lending, or tracking finances, Chamapro simplifies the entire process.

Chamapro was created to empower Chamas and investment groups by providing a modern, digital way to manage contributions and records without stress.

We believe in accountability, financial growth, and easy collaboration among members.

Our mission is to simplify group savings and contributions through technology.

We aim to ensure transparency, trust, and efficiency in every Chama.

We envision a future where Chamas and groups can operate like professional financial institutions using affordable tools.

Our vision is to make every contribution count towards financial freedom.
